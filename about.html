About Pala Coffee

Discover our story and passion for exceptional coffee

Our Story

Established in 2025, Pala Coffee Drive Thru began as a simple dream: to bring the world's finest coffee directly to your car window. Our journey started with a deep appreciation for the art of coffee making and a commitment to quality that has never wavered.

We believe that great coffee starts with great beans. That's why we've built relationships with coffee farmers across the globe, ensuring that every bean we source meets our exacting standards for quality and sustainability. Located at 2/6 Phillip Street, Dallas VIC 3047, we're the hidden gem of the northern suburbs.

Our Mission

To provide coffee lovers with an exceptional drive-thru experience through premium quality beans, sustainable practices, and a deep respect for the craft of coffee making. We're committed to supporting fair trade and environmentally responsible farming practices, while serving you quickly and conveniently every day from 6am to 8pm.
